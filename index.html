24-HOUR ASSISTANCE

Our services provide 24-hour assistance, directly to the pet sitting specialist. If you need to provide more details about your pet or simply want to check in, contacting your pet sitter is as easy as giving them a call or sending them a direct text. If you're in a different timezone, we redirect your call or text to our 24-hour Happy Pets Assistance so you never have to worry about your pet while you're on vacation.
